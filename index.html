About Us

A restaurant is a place where people go to enjoy prepared meals and beverages in exchange for payment. It serves as a social and culinary space that offers a variety of food and drink options, often catering to different tastes, dietary needs, and cultural preferences. Restaurants may range from casual and fast-service establishments to fine-dining venues, emphasizing hospitality, ambiance, and the overall dining experience.

OUR MENU

  • Beef bulalo with lemongrass and ginger

    ₱250

    Soup dishes like beef bulalo are crowd-pleasers regardless of the weather. Why not take your bulalo to the next level by introducing new aromatics to your recipe? Try lemongrass and ginger – two simple additions offering a refreshing, zesty kick. They also lend subtle citrusy notes to your soup, balancing the richness of the Pinoy favorite food and allowing the fork-tender meat to shine.

  • Crispy Chicken Fillet

    ₱150

    Upgrade your restaurant’s go-to fried chicken recipe with crispy breaded fillets featuring a Pinoy flair. Give this top Pinoy favorite food an intense meaty flavor by adding Knorr Chicken Powder at every step of the cooking process. Yes, you can also use it as a finishing seasoning! Then, serve your golden chicken fillets with fluffy ube waffles, flavored butter, and a maple cream glaze

  • Pan-grilled liempo

    ₱350

    Juicy slabs of flavorful inihaw na liempo never fail to satisfy hungry customers. The secret? A generous punch of umami goodness thanks to the addition of Knorr Liquid Seasoning in both the marinade and the sweet glaze. Serve this favorite Filipino dish on its own with a signature vinegar dipping sauce. Or, create your version of surf-and-turf party trays by pairing the grilled pork with cheesy baked tahong, steamed crabs, and garlic butter shrimp

  • Bagnet kare kare

    ₱125

    This classic recipes that call for a low-and-slow stewing process can interfere with kitchen staff efficiency. But instead of cutting the dish from your lineup, replace the usual meat with crunchy bagnet you can prepare ahead in batches. Then, add fresh, crisp vegetables to balance out the richness of the pork. Don’t forget the secret to achieving a velvety kare-kare sauce – Lady's Choice Creamy Peanut Butter!

  • Sweet & Sour Chicken

    ₱175

    Crispy golden-brown chicken pieces tossed in a vibrant, glossy sweet and sour sauce. Served with fresh bell peppers, pineapple chunks, and onions, this dish offers the perfect balance of tangy sweetness and savory flavors. Garnished with sesame seeds and a touch of fresh cilantro for a delightful finish.

  • Teriyaki Salmon

    ₱130

    A tender, perfectly seared salmon fillet glazed with a rich, glossy teriyaki sauce. Served alongside steamed white rice, sautéed broccoli, and thinly sliced carrots, this dish offers a harmonious blend of sweet, savory, and umami flavors. Garnished with toasted sesame seeds and fresh green onions for a visually appealing and delicious finish.
